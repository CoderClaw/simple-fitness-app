About Simple Fitness App

Hey there! Welcome to Simple Fitness App! I'm a solo developer who loves fitness and coding, and I created this app just for fun and to help people like you personalize their workout routines.

With Simple Fitness App, you can:

  • Create and manage your own workout plans
  • Track your progress and set goals
  • Explore a library of exercises with step-by-step guides
  • Customize exercises to suit your needs

This app is all about being simple, easy to use, and flexible, so you can stay motivated and crush your fitness goals. Whether you're just starting out or you're a fitness pro, there's something here for you.

Thanks for checking out Simple Fitness App. I hope you have as much fun using it as I did making it!
